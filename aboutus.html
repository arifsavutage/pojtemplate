Semarang Water Front Living

Project for developing an area of approximately ±300 hectares on the beach with beautiful views directly facing the Java Sea. Consisting of various activities Housing, Commercial Area, Offices, Hotels, Education, Recreation, etc. which are referred to as Township. Located in the City of West Semarang and developed by a trusted developer PT Indo Permata Usahatama. Build new "TOWNSHIP" integrated, iconic and sustainable with the best and profitable quality for the environtment, Semarang city and stock holders.

FIRST MISSION

3 Great Plans Will Be Done.

Utilizing the rich history of the maritime city of Semarang as an anchor culture.

Create a waterfront public recreation destination in Semarang Waterfront City.

Offering maximum accessibility from the project site to various tourist destinations-booth new and existing ones, mainly through waterways.

SECOND MISSION

A New Concept Of Green Marina Living.

Creating a green connecting network whole city so pedestrian access.

Integrating Semarang city essence in spatial themes such as 'Culinary Park', 'Bussiness Park', 'Nature Track'.

Promotes the adoption of sustainability that is integrated with urban development and new urban lifestyle.

THIRD MISSION

A Truly Semarang Home.

Create program variations for different ages and social clasess.

Sticking to the "Semarang Spirit" as "Cultural Melting Pot" (Cultural diversity), it shows the innovation of urban facilities in this new city.

Integrate local habits and lifestyles, at the same time promoting openness and innovation.
